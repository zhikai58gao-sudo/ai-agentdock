About AI Rules Hub

A free, curated directory of AI coding rules for modern development workflows.

What Is AI Rules Hub?

AI Rules Hub is an open directory of ready-made programming rules for AI-powered code editors such as Cursor and Windsurf. Instead of writing rules from scratch, you can browse, search, and copy templates tailored to popular stacks — Next.js, Python, Supabase, Tailwind CSS, Rust, and more.

Rules are persistent instructions that tell your AI assistant how to write code for your project: naming conventions, framework preferences, testing patterns, and other team standards. Drop them into .cursor/rules or import them in Windsurf, and your editor will follow them across every session.

Why Use It?

  • Save time — start from battle-tested rule templates instead of a blank file
  • Stay consistent — align AI output with your stack and coding style
  • One-click copy — grab a rule and paste it into your editor in seconds
  • Filter by category — find rules for frontend, backend, mobile, AI/ML, and more

Who Is It For?

AI Rules Hub is built for individual developers, freelancers, and small teams who rely on AI-assisted coding and want better, more predictable results. Whether you are bootstrapping a side project or standardizing conventions across a codebase, curated rules help your AI understand what "good code" means in your context.
