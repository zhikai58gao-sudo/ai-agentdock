Submit a Rule

Share a rule that works well for your stack and help other developers get better AI-assisted code.

What Makes a Good Submission?

  • Clear, actionable instructions an AI editor can follow consistently
  • Focused on a specific stack, framework, or workflow (e.g. Next.js App Router, FastAPI, Supabase RLS)
  • Reasonable length — concise enough to fit in a context window after trimming
  • Original or properly attributed — do not submit copyrighted content without permission

What to Include

When submitting, please provide:

  • Title — a short, descriptive name
  • Category — e.g. Frontend, Backend, Mobile, AI/ML
  • Tags — technologies covered (React, Python, Go, etc.)
  • Rule content — the full text to copy into Cursor or Windsurf
  • Brief description — one or two sentences on what the rule does
